About Us

Em Logistics was founded on January 11, 2011 in Lithuania. The company provides transportation and forwarding services throughout Europe. The main type of activity is the transportation of new and used vehicles by road transporters on international routes. Our main goal is quality. Thanks to our many years of experience, we can offer fast, safe, high-quality and reliable transportation of vehicles. We are dedicated to our work, a team of specialists who are always open, accessible and reliable to long-term partners and new customers.
